About the Project

This arena benchmarks standard Large Language Models (LLMs) against a Neuro-Symbolic approach. While LLMs excel at language, they often struggle with tight logical constraints. Our Neuro-Symbolic solver uses an LLM to parse natural language into formal logic, which is then solved with mathematical precision by a symbolic engine.
